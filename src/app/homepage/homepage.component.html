ABOUT NUELJEFF

NUELJEFF INTERNATIONAL LIMITED is a fully registered Nigerian Company which was established in 2014 with RC No: 1223364. NUELJEFF INTERNATIONAL LIMITED is a service driven oriented Company, specialized in provision of high quality service in safety and allied marketing operations. Our scope of operations ranges from procurement, maintenance and supply of top quality safety materials, oil & gas services, logistic supports, procurement and supply of industrial/Agro materials and general contracts.


Here at NUELJEFF INTERNATIONAL LIMITED, Safety consciousness is an essential integral part of our activities and no effort is spared in ensuring that this concept is enshrined in our operations.

OUR MISSION

Committing to excellence, maintain the trust and confidence of our customers, and principals. Conducting business ethically with a world class standard of safety and quality.

OUR VISION

NUELJEFF INTERNATIONAL LIMITED’s vision is to be a regional pioneer company and a benchmark for providing best-in class services.

Quality, Health, Safety and Environmental (QHSE) Policy

We are committed to achieving customer satisfaction in all our services, through promotion, maintenance and facilitation of continual improvement in our quality and safety systems.
